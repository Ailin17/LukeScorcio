Luke has studied improv at UCB Los Angeles and iO West. He performs regularly on the team Pretty Ugly which is coached by Brian James O’Connell. Back in Oshkosh, Luke was one of the original improv team members at The Backlot Comedy House and was the creator of The Adventures of Sammy the Space Slug.
